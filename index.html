FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BRB Screen?

BRB Screen is a free tool that automatically plays Twitch clips during your stream breaks. It keeps your audience entertained while you're away and can display clips from your own channel or any other Twitch channels.

How do I add this to OBS?

Add a Browser Source in OBS, paste your generated URL, and set the dimensions to 1920x1080 (or your stream resolution). Create a scene with this source and switch to it when you go on break.

Can I use clips from multiple channels?

Yes! Enter multiple channel names separated by commas. The clips will be shuffled together for variety. Great for collaborations or showcasing your favorite streamers.

Is this free? What's the catch?

It's completely free with no catch. BRB Screen is a community project powered by IRLServer.com. We don't track you, sell data, or require accounts.

Will clips repeat during my break?

Our smart shuffle algorithm prevents back-to-back repeats and prioritizes variety. Clips are weighted by popularity and recency to show your best content.

Can I customize the appearance?

Yes! You can adjust volume, show/hide clip info overlays, add countdown timers, and more. The URL contains all your settings, so save it for future use.
